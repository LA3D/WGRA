WGRA Nuggets

Browsable bites on the ideas behind the Wiki-Grounded Research Agent

One idea per nugget: short, self-contained, and browsable in any order. The flyer is the full pitch and the deep dives go long; these sit in between, a set of concepts you can graze rather than a story you follow start to finish.

Start anywhere. If you want the lineage, Karpathy’s llm-wiki is where the whole thing begins.

Decentralized identifiers

identity

Online identity today is issued and controlled by platforms; a decentralized identifier is one you own and can prove, and it is what would keep the federation’s discovery open while access stays gated.
